    About Us

    Diyan Universal School, Was Started In July 2019 aim to provide a liberal, happy and diverse environment for students that not only nurtures their creativity and enthuses finer values like empathy, integrity, patriotism and honesty but also helps them realise their true potential and eventually they evolve as global leaders with character.

    Founded By

    Eksangh Samajik, Shaikshanik Krida & Sanskratik Mandal

    The School

    The name Diyan signifies 'Bright Light. As such, Diyan Universal School aspires to provide education to students in a manner that they grow up to become guiding lights; by radiating their brightness into the society. We endeavour to prepare future global citizens, who will be trend setters, lead by example, contribute to their country, be instruments of social change and above all become good human beings.

    Diyan has been incepted by a passionate and dedicated team of educationists, committed to the cause of providing quality education to little and young minds. The school offers classes for Pre-Primary Section (i.e. Play Group, Nursery, Junior Kindergarten and Senior Kindergarten) and Primary Section (from Grade -I onwards) with classes increasing as per natural growth every year. It will eventually have classes from Play Group to Grade 10 over the years.

    The school follows the CBSE curriculum with a strong pedagogy while taking care of the psychological aspects of child development. Parents can thus, trust their children to us, knowing that your little ones will embark on a wonderful journey of learning, exploration and self-discovery; emerging at the end as holistic citizens and serve as beacons to the society and community
